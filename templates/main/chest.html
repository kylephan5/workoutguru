Barbell Bench Press

The barbell bench press is a classic exercise that targets the entirety of the chest, with an emphasis on the middle of the chest. It is the quintessential "push" compound exercise, as it engages multiple muscle groups including the triceps and front deltoids.

- - -

Typically, you want to start your workout with the bench press, as it is a pretty taxing exercise. If you are looking to put on more mass, opt for heavier weight with less reps

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 6-12
Strength Training 3-5

- - -

Incline Bench Press

The incline bench press is a compound exercise that targets the upper chest. It is a variation of the classic flat bench press. In addition, it secondarily targets similar muscles as the barbell bench press, but this time with more emphasis on the front deltoids (shoulders).

- - -

Typically, you want to start your workout with the bench press, as it is a pretty taxing exercise. If you are looking to put on more mass, opt for heavier weight with less reps

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 6-12
Strength Training 3-5

- - -

Landmine Chest Press

The landmine chest press is a more niche exercise, and targets the upper chest. It simultaneously works the middle chest and deltoid muscles. Be sure to embrace your core to help with stabilizing your body.

- - -

With this exercise, you will definitely want to increase the volume and decrease the weight. Control the weight with each rep, and focus on TIME UNDER TENSION. But, remember, that if you are looking to put on more mass, opt for heavier weight with less reps

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 6-12
Strength Training 3-5

- - -

Decline Chest Press

The decline barbell bench press is an exercise that targets the lower chest. It is a great compound exercise that engages the shoulders as well as the triceps in the movement. Similar to the incline bench press, it is a derivative of the flat bench press.

- - -

The decline bench press is an exercise that one can use for hypertrophy (high volume) or for strength/compound lifting. If you are looking to put on more mass, opt for heavier weight with less reps

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 6-12
Strength Training 3-5

- - -

Dumbbell Chest Press

The dumbbell chest press is a classic exercise that targets the entirety of the chest, with an emphasis on the middle of the chest. Similar to the barbell chest press, this exercise utilizes a pushing motion to build the chest muscles. The key for this exercise is to make sure that your elbows are not flared out when doing the motion, as it could cause shoulder pain.

- - -

For this exercise, we recommend that you aim for hypertrophy. Your goal should be able to do 8-12 solid repetitions. Your last few reps should be taxing.

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 8-12
Strength Training 3-5

- - -

Close Grip Bench Press

The close grip dumbbell bench press is an exercise that emphasis the inner chest, and triceps. You can perform this exercise on a decline, flat, or incline bench. It is a great workout to practice mind muscle connection.

Intended Goal Amount of Reps

Underhand Pectoral Fly

The underhand pectoral fly is a great warmup and also a great exercise to target the mid-chest. This exercise would be great to pair with the incline bench press (or any other compound movement).

- - -

For this exercise, we recommend that you aim for hypertrophy. Your goal should be able to do 10-15 solid repetitions. Your last few reps should be taxing. Use relatively light weight, avoid using momentum to raise the dumbbells.

- - -

Flat/Incline Pectoral Fly

The pectoral fly on a bench is a great exercise that mimics the flies performed with cables. Be sure to use relatively light weight to reduce the risk of shoulder injury. Keep a slight bend in your elbows while performing the movement. This exercise can be performed on a flat/incline bench; they will recruit different muscles depending on the angle of the bench.

- - -

For this exercise, we recommend that you aim for hypertrophy. Your goal should be able to do 8-12 solid repetitions. Your last few reps should be taxing.

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 8-12
Strength Training 3-5

Incline Dumbbell Bench Press

The incline dumbbell bench press is a similar movement to the incline barbell bench press. It targets similar muscles, but places less emphasis on the "compound" nature of the barbell and singles out each arm. Push yourself with this workout, and be sure to not flare your elbows out!

For this exercise, we recommend that you aim for hypertrophy. Your goal should be able to do 8-12 solid repetitions. Your last few reps should be taxing.

Dumbbell Pullover

The dumbbell pullover is an excellent exercise that targets the chest and the lats simultaneously. It is a unique movement that is not seen in many other exercises. It's a great exercise to incorporate in an Arnold Split (one that pairs chest/black together on the same day). Perform this exercise with relatively lighter weight to minimize the risk of shoulder injury.

- - -

For this exercise, we recommend that you aim for hypertrophy. Your goal should be able to do 8-12 solid repetitions. Your last few reps should be taxing.

Intended Goal Amount of Reps
Muscle Growth 8-12
Strength Training 3-5
